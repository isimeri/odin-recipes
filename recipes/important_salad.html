The Important Salad

Description

I'm not sure this salad has an official name, so I'm gonna call it "Important Salad". Also I couldn't find a picture of it, so I put a picture that depicts my feelings about this salad.

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Cauliflower
  • Bell pepper
  • Onion
  • Arugula
  • Cucumber
  • Parsley
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Apple cider vinegar or lime juice
  • Salt
  • Whatever spices you like(Optional)

Steps

  1. Chop all the vegetables in a big bowl
  2. Add olive oil and vinegar or lime juice
  3. Add salt and whatever other spices you decided to go with.
  4. GG, you got yourself an Important Salad. It contains lots of fiber and almost no carbs. Your liver and your weighing scale will thank you.
