Pizza

Description

This pizza is epic. It is the best. The only thing better than this pizza is multiple pizzas like this.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato paste
  • Bell pepper
  • Olives
  • Onion
  • Chicken meat
  • Mozzarella
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Make the dough nice and round and flat with the rolling pin thingy and put it in the baking tray
  2. Chop the chicken meat into tiny pieces and fry them real good in lard for a little bit
  3. Take that tomato paste and spread it nicely all over that dough
  4. Throw the fried chicken meat on top
  5. Chop the vegetables and the olives and throw them on top of the pizza as well
  6. The pizza needs a little bit of salt at this stage probably
  7. Grate the mozzarella on top of everything else. Make sure it covers the whole pizza
  8. Set the oven to a resonable temperature and let your work of art stay there for a reasonable amount of time
  9. Enjoy your glorious pizza. It looks epic for sure. If it doesn't, next time it will be great 100%
