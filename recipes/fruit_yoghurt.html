Healthy Fruit Yoghurt Dessert

Description

The picture is just for reference. The final product will probably not look like this.

Ingredients

  • Pure yoghurt, without sugar, fruit or any of that stuff
  • A handful or two of berries, e.g. strawberry, raspberry, blueberry, blackberry etc.
  • Some nice seeds or nuts, e.g. pumpkin seeds, pecan nuts, walnuts etc<./li>
  • Stevia or some other sweetener that doesn't make your insulin go brrr(Optional)

Steps

  1. Use a blender or something to turn the berries into mush
  2. Add the youghurt on top of the berry mush
  3. Throw in the seeds and nuts
  4. Stir fiercely
  5. If you like it as is, very nice! If it's meh, add some of that sweetener.
  6. Enjoy your dessert. Your neighbours will look at your waist and think "Now that's a very nice waist, I want a waist like that"
